Robot Morphology Survey

In this survey, you will optimize the design and placement of visual sensors on a robot for it to complete a target navigation task.

There will be 3 "levels" of questions: 1 level with only a textual decription of an arbitrary environment, and 2 levels with a textual description along with a render of a home-like environment

In each level, there will be 3 questions: 1 question about placing cameras, and 2 questions about placing photoreceptors.


Please make sure you are taking this survey on laptop on a Chromium browser.
