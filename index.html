Robot Morphology Survey

In this survey, you will optimize the design and placement of visual sensors on a robot for it to complete a target navigation task.

There will be 2 "levels" of questions: one with only a textual decription of the environment, and one with a textual description along with a render of the environment

In each level, there will be 4 questions: 1 question about placing cameras, and 3 questions about placing photoreceptors.
